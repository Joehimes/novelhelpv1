About Us

Finally finished your books and not sure where next to turn?

Don’t want to give up control of your book?

Frustrated by the process of finding a traditional agent and publisher?

Welcome to Novel Help

Novel Help is uniquely designed to assist writers with as much…or as little of the publishing process as is desired. The options for publishing are dizzying. Just Google Self Publishing and you will quickly see too many options to consider and too much information to process.

This is where Novel Help comes in. We provide a menu of items to assist you in the publishing process…from simple publishing process advice to complete formatting, cover design, uploading and eBook conversion services.

And, Novel Help lets you keep all of your book rights and royalties.
